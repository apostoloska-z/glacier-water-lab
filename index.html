From Glacier to Glass: Clean Water Through Filtration & Conservation

Glacier water may look clear, but it can still contain sediment and microorganisms. This site explains our filtration experiment, shows how water quality improves, and teaches why saving clean water is essential (SDG 6).

Glacier water often contains sediment and impurities that aren't visible to the naked eye.

A simple physical filtration model was built using several cups, each filled with a different filter layer:
